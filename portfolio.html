Wonderful Indonesia Memory Games

Wonderful Indonesia is a very simple memory game that aims to promote Indonesia tourism especially some of 10 Bali Baru. You will be given 12 cards that showcase 6 tourism destinations in Indonesia including Borobudur Temple, Mount Bromo, Labuan Bajo, Lake Toba, Morotai Island, and Wakatobi. You will get points after finding a matches card.
