Rasa Sayange

Rasa Sayange is a Maluku folksong which its literal meaning is "loving feeling". This arrangement is created for string quartet and combo. This arrangement is commisioned by Gadjah Mada Chamber Orchestra and have been performed in many events.

Tomorrow (from Annie)

Tomorrow is gratest hitz from the movie Annie. This song tell a story about how we should see the future as a bright and positive thing. This arrangement is created for mini orchestra and solo vocal

Medley Indonesia

Medley Indonesia is a medley of songs including Mbiring Manggis from North Sumatera, Mande Mande (Moluccan Folksong), and Cik Cik Periuk from Borneo. This song was originally arranged by M. Ginting for choir. I created the string quintet adaptation for Gadjah Mada Chamber Orchestra to be performed in many events.
